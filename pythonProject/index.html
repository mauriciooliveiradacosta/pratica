Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a popular dark fantasy anime and manga series that follows the story of humanity's struggle for survival against giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The series is known for its intense action, deep themes, and complex characters.

Main Characters

Eren Jaeger

Eren is the protagonist of the series. He is driven by a desire for revenge against the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa is Eren's childhood friend and adoptive sister. She is one of the strongest soldiers in the Survey Corps and is fiercely protective of Eren.

Armin Arlert

Armin is Eren's childhood friend and a skilled strategist. He often serves as the voice of reason among the trio.
